About

Making video clipping effortless.

VidSnap was built for creators, editors, and anyone who's ever thought "that part would make a great short." Instead of scrubbing through hours of footage, let AI find the moments that matter.

Powered by Gemini AI and yt-dlp, VidSnap analyzes transcripts to identify the most engaging segments — then downloads them as vertical clips ready for YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, or TikTok.

Built with Django, styled with Tailwind CSS, and designed with care.
